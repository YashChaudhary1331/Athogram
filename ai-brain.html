01

📋 Personalized Training Plan Generator

Creates a full multi-week plan based on your goals (weight loss, muscle gain, endurance, sport performance, etc.), fitness level (beginner → elite), available equipment, and weekly schedule. Plans include day-by-day exercises with sets/reps/duration and milestone targets.

02

🍽️ Meal Photo Analysis

Upload a photo of any meal — the coach analyzes nutrition content, estimates macros (protein/carbs/fat/calories), and tells you whether it supports your training goals.

03

🎬 Training Form Analysis

Upload a workout video and get specific technique feedback — the coach identifies form errors and gives correction cues for your exact exercise.

04

🎙️ Voice Coach (12 Languages)

Full voice interaction — tap and hold to speak, Sarvam AI converts your speech to text, Claude processes it, and the coach speaks back. Works in English, Hindi, Hinglish, and 10 other Indian languages.

05

🔔 Proactive Monitoring & Alerts

The coach silently watches your activity in the background. It scans your posts for injury/fatigue signals, checks training logs for overtraining or missed workouts, monitors wearable HRV and sleep data, and proactively starts a conversation when it detects a concern — without you having to ask.

06

💪 Weekly Performance Check-In

On demand review of your entire week — training volume, sleep scores, HRV trends, personal bests hit, and a concrete focus for the coming week.

07

🩹 Injury Consultation

Dedicated injury advice mode. Describe the pain, location, and how it happened — the coach helps you understand what it might be, what to do, and when to see a professional.

08

🎯 Customizable Coaching Style

Five personalities to choose from: Motivational (high energy), Analytical (data-driven), Balanced (default), Strict (tough love), Gentle (supportive). Applies consistently across all conversations.

09

📚 RAG Knowledge Base

The coach answers questions using a curated sports science knowledge base (exercise, nutrition, recovery, sport technique, injury prevention, mental health) — not just general LLM knowledge. Answers are grounded in verified athletic content.

10

💬 Persistent Multi-Topic Chat

Separate chat threads per topic (workout planning, nutrition, recovery, goal setting, progress review, injury advice) with full history, context summaries between sessions, and a floating "Ask Coach" button accessible from anywhere.
